About


Blender generates custom playlists and adds them straight to users' Spotify account, using Spotify's own recommendation system. Users can choose up to 5 seeds, from artists, tracks or songs, which are then 'blended' into a playlist.


I made this app as I wanted to use Spotify's recommendation capabilities, which aren't being used in the main Spotify app. This app is different to Spotify radio, as it doesn't specifically select songs you listen to a lot, creating a more fresh listening experience. It also offers more than Spotify's recommended songs feature, as you can easily create a whole playlist of songs, with your custom seeds with my app.
